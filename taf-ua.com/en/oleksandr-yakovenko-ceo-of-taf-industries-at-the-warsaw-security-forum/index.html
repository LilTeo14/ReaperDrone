Oleksandr Yakovenko, CEO of TAF Industries, at the Warsaw Security Forum on how Ukraine can strengthen its defense complex through strategic cooperation:

Joint Ventures: European investments and technologies to scale up Ukrainian production.
Localization: Creating sustainable supply chains to make our military-industrial complex independent and fast.
Export: Opening EU markets for Ukrainian weapons is about financial strength and the development of innovation.
These are concrete steps that will allow Ukraine to become a technological leader and a full-fledged player in the European defense market.
