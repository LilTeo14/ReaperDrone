Next-Level Secure Communication

Kolibri 13” Advance operates on MilELRS firmware, ensuring stable performance under intense EW conditions. Support for non-standard video frequencies allows the drone to adapt to specific mission requirements

EW Resistance

Dual control bands with automatic in-flight channel switching for uninterrupted operation

MilELRS Firmware

Optimizes radio modules, extends control range, increases resistance to interference, and guarantees stable signal transmission

Non-Standard Video Frequencies

Available frequencies range from 1.1 GHz to 7.1 GHz

High-Performance Optics for Any Scenario

Kolibri 13” Advance can be equipped with different camera types, ensuring explicit imagery both day and night, across a wide range of combat environments

Analog Camera

Reliable daytime video transmission. A cost-effective choice for rapid target engagement

Thermal Camera

Enhanced night vision and target acquisition. Increases operational effectiveness during low-visibility and night missions

Digital Camera

High-resolution video with encrypted transmission. Provides detailed imagery with maximum protection against interception

Turning DRONES INTO A POWERFUL WEAPON

Functionality designed with direct military feedback to maximize strike precision and mission success

“Last Mile” AI-Guided Targeting System

An AI-powered system that automatically corrects drone trajectory towards the target, even under EW suppression, poor visibility, or evasive maneuvers by the object

“Powerkey” UAV Delayed Start System

Allows remote drone activation, minimizing operator risks and ensuring rapid deployment without physical contact

“TAF-P2” Initiation Board

Ground Control Station
